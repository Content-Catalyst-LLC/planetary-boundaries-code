# Methodological Notes

This workflow uses the Doughnut logic of ecological ceiling and social foundation.

## Ecological overshoot

Overshoot is calculated as:

O = max(0, (observed pressure - ecological ceiling) / ecological ceiling)

## Social shortfall

Shortfall is calculated as:

Q = max(0, (social floor - observed achievement) / social floor)

## Interpretation

The combined score is only a diagnostic aid. It should not be interpreted as a final measure of sustainability, justice, or development performance. Ecological thresholds may be nonlinear, social foundations may be rights-based, and different indicators may not be substitutable.
